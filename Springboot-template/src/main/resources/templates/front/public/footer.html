MIN DE ENERGY

Mingde Oil & Gas Co., Ltd. is an energy company founded in 2005 and headquartered in Beijing, China. The company's principal activities are the exploration, extraction and sale of oil and natural gas. The company is committed to providing sustainable energy solutions for the global energy market, is a leading technology, environmental protection, global vision and social responsibility of the energy company, to provide sustainable energy solutions for the global energy market, but also committed to achieve its own sustainable development.
